Stratofall

Stratofall was a game I made for my independent study project on Android programming. You play as a skydiver whos goal is to fall as far as possible dodging lightning clouds and popping balloons along the way. You control the skydiver by tilting your phone either left or right. The more balloons you pop before you run out of lives the higher your score will be.

Created with Java and libGDX. Runs on Android.
